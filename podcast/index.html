HSBK Podcast


Keeping you informed on the latest data mining and privacy issues!

Sam Roquitte, Hallie Byatt, Katie Groenhout, and Bora Edis

Our Mission

The HSBK podcast informs and discusses topics related to data mining and privacy. Accurately inform social media users about the secret invasions of personal privacy that corporations use to make cash money.

Meet the Team

Host: Sam Roquitte is an animated and humorous person with interests in difficult controversial topics. He is pursuing a degree in Computer Science at the Georgia Institute of Technology with a concentration in online security. He is originally from Madison, Wisconsin. Sam loves oatmeal raisin cookies.

Writer: Hallie Byatt is pursuing a degree in Mathematics at the Georgia Institute of Technology. She is originally from Houston, Texas. Hallie runs on coffee.

Writer: Katie Groenhout is pursuing a degree in Chemical Engineering at Georgia Tech. She is originally from Charlotte, North Carolina. She has two dogs and loves popcorn.

Editor: Bora Edis is an Industrial Engineering student at Georgia Tech. He is originally from Turkey but grew up in Dubai. He has had a variety of experience with digital audio editing over the last couple of years working of projects like music videos. Bora’s favorite dessert is Tiramisu.
